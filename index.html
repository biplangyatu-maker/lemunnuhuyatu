About

From SME financing to the lecture hall

Dr Lemun Yatu holds a PhD in Business Management with a focus on entrepreneurship and enterprise development in emerging economies. He joined the University of Bedfordshire Business School as a Senior Tutor in Business Management and coordinator for Teaching & Learning at the Birmingham Study Centre.

T

Teaching Philosophy

A Fellow of the Higher Education Academy, Lemun believes in experiential, learn-by-doing education — giving students room to challenge convention and apply theory to real business problems.

C

Prior Roles

Previously at De Montfort University, Birmingham City University Business School and the University of Worcester Business School, plus a UNIDO consultancy on entrepreneurial capacity development across sub-Saharan Africa.
